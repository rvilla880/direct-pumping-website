Why Regular Service
Is Non-Negotiable

⚖️

Health Code Compliance

Municipal health codes require regular grease trap maintenance. Failure to comply can result in fines, citations, or forced closure.

🚫

Prevent Sewer Blockages

Overloaded traps release FOG into the sewer system, causing backups in your lines — and liability for municipal system damage.

👃

Eliminate Odors

Grease buildup produces strong odors that affect kitchen staff, dining guests, and neighboring businesses. Regular pumping keeps it in check.
